## Pilot Purpose
Evaluate OpenPlan for grant-support corridor analysis workflows (e.g., ATP, SS4A, RAISE, PROTECT) using real agency corridors and publicly available datasets.

## Scope (Included)
1. Pilot kickoff meeting (60 minutes)
2. Up to **3 corridor analyses** during pilot period
3. Per-corridor deliverables:

   - Methods & Assumptions summary

4. Midpoint review (30 minutes)
5. Closeout meeting (45 minutes) + adoption recommendation memo

- Final legal/regulatory sign-off
- Full grant narrative authorship/submission management
- Custom model development beyond pilot workflow
- Integrations requiring separate engineering scoping
