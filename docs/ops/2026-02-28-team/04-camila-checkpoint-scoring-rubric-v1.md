# Camila Checkpoint Scoring Rubric v1

- **Owner:** Owen (Associate Planner)
- **Reviewer:** Elena (Principal Planner)
- **Use:** +24h and +48h design checkpoint scoring

## Scoring Scale
- **2 = PASS-READY**
- **1 = PARTIAL / NEEDS EDIT**
- **0 = MISSING / BLOCKING**

## Criteria
1. **Visual Clarity**
   - Layout hierarchy is understandable in <5 seconds
   - Primary actions are visually obvious

2. **System Consistency**
   - Components use consistent spacing/type/color logic
   - Variant/state naming is coherent

3. **Accessibility Intent**
   - Contrast decisions and focus states are documented
   - Risk areas are explicitly called out

4. **Implementation Readiness**
   - Tokens and component specs are usable by engineering
   - No ambiguous behavior/state definitions

5. **Demo Readiness**
   - Top screens look polished for stakeholder preview
   - Brand treatment is coherent and professional

## Gate Rules
- **PASS:** total >= 8 and no 0 in criteria 3 or 4
- **PASS-WITH-EDITS:** total 6-7 and no more than one 0
- **HOLD:** total <= 5 or any blocking 0 in criteria 3 or 4
